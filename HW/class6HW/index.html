Do Your Ideas Exceed Your Abilities?

At Precious Metal Arts, we specialize in helping individuals realize their artistic goals, whether you want to make a single piece or design an entire line of jewelry.

We offer jewelry classes and workshops covering all fundamental aspects of jewelry design and creation, and provide customized continuing education through our unique small group tutoring. We even offer bench rental for you to practice your developing skills at a professional jeweler’s bench as you learn, without having to invest hundreds of dollars in tools.
